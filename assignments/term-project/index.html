In the 178th Annual General Conference of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints. Newly Called Pres Thomas S. Monson announces two new temples to be built in Arizona. One of them being The Gilbert Temple, located in Gilbert, Arizona. This would be the fourth opperating temple to be located in Arizona. The other three is The Mesa Arizona Temple (1927), the Snowflake Arizona Temple (2002), and The Gila Valley Arizona Temple (2010).
